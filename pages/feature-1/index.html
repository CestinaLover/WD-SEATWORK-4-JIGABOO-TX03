BATMAN: 

ARKHAM KNIGHT 

Rocksteady Studios // WB Games Montréal

Batman: Arkham Knight is an action-adventure game set within an open world Gotham City, which can be explored freely by the player from the beginning of the game, allowing them to travel seamlessly anywhere within its boundaries.

PLAY NOW

Nightwing

Nightwing, formerly known as Robin, is the original sidekick of Batman. After his parents were tragically killed in a circus accident, Bruce Wayne took him in and trained him to fight crime. Eventually, Dick outgrew his role as Robin and took on the new identity of Nightwing, becoming a hero in his own right. In the Arkham Knight game, Nightwing is a key ally of Batman, providing support both in combat and intelligence gathering.

Robin

Tim Drake is the third individual to take on the mantle of Robin. Unlike his predecessors, Tim deduced Batman's secret identity and volunteered to become the new Robin after the death of Jason Todd, the second Robin. Tim's intellect and detective skills make him a valuable partner to Batman. In the Arkham Knight game, Robin works closely with Batman to take down the villains threatening Gotham City.

Oracle

Barbara Gordon, once known as Batgirl, is the daughter of Gotham City Police Commissioner James Gordon. After being paralyzed by the Joker, Barbara adopted the identity of Oracle. From her wheelchair, she provides crucial information and technological support to Batman and his allies. In the Arkham Knight game, Oracle is an essential part of Batman's team, helping to coordinate missions and gather intel.

From the fearsome Scarecrow and the mysterious Arkham Knight to the chaotic Joker and the ruthless Penguin, players face a rogues' gallery of iconic threats. Each villain presents unique challenges as Batman battles to save his city from ultimate destruction.

Scarecrow

Once a respected psychologist, Dr. Jonathan Crane turned into the sinister Scarecrow, using fear as a weapon. In "Arkham Knight," he orchestrates a plan to unleash a new, terrifying fear toxin across Gotham, forcing Batman to confront his darkest fears.

Arkham Knight

The Arkham Knight is a mysterious, militaristic adversary with a deep-seated grudge against Batman. Equipped with advanced technology and a formidable army, his knowledge of Batman's tactics and secrets makes him a formidable foe.

Joker

The Clown Prince of Crime, Joker, continues to torment Batman posthumously through a hallucinogenic infection. His chaotic presence haunts Batman, symbolizing their intertwined fates and the constant battle between order and chaos.

Harley Quinn

Former psychiatrist Harleen Quinzel, now Harley Quinn, is a psychotic and unpredictable villainess. Devoted to Joker, she leads her own gang in "Arkham Knight," proving to be a persistent and dangerous threat to Batman and Gotham.
              
              

BATMAN'S GADGETS

Rocksteady Studios // WB Games Montréal

In "Batman: Arkham City," Batman's extensive array of high-tech gadgets enhances his combat and detective abilities. From the Batarang and Batclaw to the Cryptographic Sequencer and Smoke Pellet, each gadget plays a crucial role in navigating the city, solving puzzles, and taking down enemies. Discover how these iconic tools make Batman the ultimate vigilante.
