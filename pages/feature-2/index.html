SATISFACTORY 

Coffee Stain Studios // Founded in 2010

"Satisfactory" is a first-person open-world factory building game with a dash of exploration and combat. As an engineer working for FICSIT Inc., you are dropped onto an alien planet with a mission to construct massive, automated factories to produce various goods and explore the vast terrain. Harness the planet's natural resources, build complex conveyor belt networks, and optimize production lines to achieve maximum efficiency. Dive into a rich and immersive world where creativity meets industry, and embark on a journey of endless possibilities and innovation.

Doggo

The Lizard Doggo is a short reptilian dog-like creature, which can be tamed by the pioneer. It is widely viewed as the game's mascot after its initial appearance in the teaser trailer and was the first and only thing revealed about the game until the E3 Reveal Trailer. Internally it used to be referred to as "Shrimpy", or "Space Rabbit", but Coffee Stain Studios have since adopted the community-given name "Lizard Doggo" internally.

Hog

The Fluffy-tailed Hog (a.k.a 'Hog'), is a hostile melee creature that the Pioneer can encounter while exploring. It attacks by charging like a bull, dealing damage with a knockback effect. It is among the most common creatures found in the game world.

Spitter

The Plasma Spitter, or Spitter, is a class of ranged hostile creature that deals damage with its fireball attack. It can be seen in groups, guarding the mid-tier resource patches such as Coal.
